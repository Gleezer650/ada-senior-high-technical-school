About ADA SENIOR HIGH TECHNICAL SCHOOL

Excellence in Technical Education Since 1991

Our Background

The Education Reform Programme of 1987 brought in its wake the establishment of Senior Secondary Schools in the country to add to the existing ones. The then Dangme East District Assembly embraced the concept and accepted the challenge.

Sege, a rapidly developing town, some 76km from Accra and some 39km from Ada foah on the main Accra-Afloa road, was selected. The argument at the time was that Ada-Foah and Big Ada were already blessed with a Teacher Training College, Technical Institute and a Secondary School.

The history of Ada Senior High Technical School will not be complete without mentioning names like Mr. Alex Tettey-Enyo (the then District Secretary of Dangme East), the late Aaron Kicher, the late J.M.T Dosoo and Mr. Tetteh Akwadah.

The new school which was christened Ada Secondary Technical School was officially commissioned by Mr. Amos Buertey (M.P for Ada) on February 22, 1991 with 110 students and 5 teachers.

First Admissions

Seventy-five (75) boys and thirty-five (35) girls were enrolled as pioneer students of ASTECH to pursue various programmes namely: Agricultural, Science, Technical and Vocational.

Our Founding Principles

  • The best use of the minds and bodies which God has given us.
  • An equal opportunity for boys and girls.
  • Respect for all that is true and of lasting value in the old African culture, belief, and way of life.
  • Willing, humble service of the educated for the uneducated.
  • Mutual understanding and cooperation between Christians of all denominations.
  • Friendship, respect, and cooperation among all races on equal terms.

Our Vision

To educate learners to be effective and efficient in the world of work for national development.

Our Mission

To equip learners with requisite skills, knowledge, attitude through learner centered quality teaching and learning in a conducive environment, taking into consideration inclusivity and stakeholders participation for national development.
